This Privacy Policy describes how the operator of this Family Call Center installation (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our voicemail and related telephone services (the “Service”).

Information We Collect

When you call our number or interact with the Service, we may collect:

  • Telephone numbers — your caller ID and any numbers you dial or provide.
  • Voicemail recordings — audio messages you leave, including duration and timestamp.
  • Transcripts — text generated from voicemail audio when transcription is enabled.
  • Call metadata — such as call time, duration, and technical identifiers supplied by our telephony provider.

If you are an authorized administrator, we also process login credentials and activity related to managing messages and settings.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Receive, store, and deliver voicemail messages to authorized recipients.
  • Display caller information and transcripts in the admin interface.
  • Send optional SMS notifications about new messages when configured.
  • Operate, secure, and maintain the Service.
  • Block unwanted callers when a blocklist is in use.

Third-Party Services

The Service relies on third-party providers, including Twilio for telephony, recording, transcription, and messaging. Information you provide through a phone call may be processed by those providers according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Storage and Retention

Voicemail audio, transcripts, and related metadata are stored on systems controlled by the operator of this installation. Retention periods depend on how the Service is configured and when messages are deleted by an administrator. We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect stored information, including access controls for the admin interface. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

If you do not wish to leave a voicemail, you may end the call before recording begins. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of information associated with your call, contact the operator of this Service using the contact details they have made available.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about this installation, please contact the operator of the Service at the address or phone number associated with the number you called.
